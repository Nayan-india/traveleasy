Jaipur: The Pink City

3D/2N
Visitor attractions include the Albert Hall Museum, Hawa Mahal, Jal Mahal, City Palace, Amer Fort, Jantar Mantar, Nahargarh Fort, Jaigarh Fort and many more.
Booking Price: 5000 Rs

Jodhpur: The Blue City

3D/2N
Mehrangarh Fort which overlooks upon the city, the blue bylanes of the old city, Umaid Bhawan Palace, Jaswant Thada, and the Ghanta Ghar, or Clock Tower. Thar Desert starts here.
Booking Price: 5000 Rs

Udaipur & Ajmer

3D/2N
Udaipur has received a rich cultural heritage from the bygone ages. The lakes, temples, huge forts, and palaces boast about the rich legacy of this city. Attractions are City Palace, Lake Palace, Monsoon Palace, Jag Mandir and many more.
Booking Price: 5000 Rs

Bikaner & Jaisalmer

3D/2N
The city is most famous for its savoury snack Bikaneri bhujia which is sold all over the world. Visitor's attractions are Junagarh Fort, Laxmi Niwas Palace, Rao Bikaji's Fort, camel ride in Thar Desert and many more.
Booking Price: 5000 Rs

The Marwar

7D/6N
The heart of Rajasthan where lies the Thar Desert and Old & Robust Forts of Rajasthan. Districts covered : Jodhpur, Jaisalmer, Ajmer, Nagaur, Bikaner, Barmer.
Booking Price: 10000 Rs

The Mewar

7D/6N
The land of Raj Palaces and oldes mountain ranges of India (The Aravalies). Districts covered: Udaipur, Rajsamand, Sirohi, Pratapghar, Bhilwara, Bundi.
Booking Price: 10000 Rs

Kota & Ranthambore

5D/4N
Ranthambore National Park is one of the largest national parks in India. Some tourist attractions in Kota ara Zoological Park, Maharao Madho Singh Museum, Kota Government Museum, Brijraj Bhawan Palace, Abheda Mahal, Hanging Rock Fountain.
Booking Price: 7000 Rs

The Shekhawati

5D/4N
Come and feel the architecture of Shekhawati region. Havelis and Frescos and the hospitality of people. Districts covered: Sikar, Churu, Jhunjunu, Hanumangarh.
Booking Price: 6000 Rs
